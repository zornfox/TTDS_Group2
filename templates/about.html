Welcome to our Coronavirus Fake News Search Tool!

Social media brings communities together around the world, and its daily use has skyrocketed. As we spend more and more time browsing, commenting, sharing, and interacting with social media posts, our consumption of news via social media snippets has become the norm. However, with greater connectivity comes great consequences… These snippets of information are so easily accessible, quickly shared, and believable, it can often be difficult to determine falsehood from reality.

The current Covid-19 pandemic has also been called the “Pandemic of Fake News”, due to the fast spreading of misinformation regarding alternative “cures” for the virus, many of which had no scientific backing, and were in fact quite dangerous methods. This is where this search engine comes in. With this tool you can search any covid related claim, and our system will return a clear list of scientific papers, news articles, and other relevant information to help you to “fact-check” your claim.
